Removing Roles

Either:

► In the User Roles tree, click Roles.

A list of current roles appears on the right of the screen.

► Click remove on the line for the role you want to remove.

► Confirm the request when prompted to do so.

Or:

► In the User Roles tree, expand the Roles branch and select the role you want to remove.

► Click remove role in the frame on the right of the page.

► Confirm the request when prompted to do so. As noted previously, you should accept the option to remove the role from DPM.
